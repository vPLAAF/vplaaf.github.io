# 1. Purpose of Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the virtual People’s Liberation Army Air Force (vPLAAF) collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in accordance with the **EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, a copy of which is available [here](https://gdpr-info.eu/).

vPLAAF is a non-profit virtual aviation organization operating on the VATSIM network.
We only collect data necessary for membership management and operational safety.

# 2. Applicability

This policy applies to:

- All current vPLAAF members
- All former members whose data remains stored under the allowed retention period
- All applicants and prospective members
- Any individual interacting with vPLAAF systems (website, Discord, training platform)

# 3. Lawful Bases for Processing

vPLAAF processes personal data on the following legal bases:

- **Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a))**
   Granted when you apply for membership or submit information voluntarily.
- **Legitimate Interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))**
   Required to verify identity, maintain safety, support operations, and comply with VSOA policies.

Members may withdraw consent at any time.

# 4. Data We Collect

vPLAAF collects only the minimum data required for identity verification, training, and operational participation.

### 4.1 Personal Data

- First & Last Name (or First name + initial, upon request)
- Email Address
- VATSIM CID
- Discord Username / Discord User ID
- Training records (hours, checkride results, qualification status)
- vPLAAF callsign and flight activity (public VATSIM data)

### 4.2 Data NOT Collected

To minimize risk, vPLAAF does **not** collect:

- Phone numbers
- Government ID numbers
- Residential addresses
- Facial or biometric data
- Payment or financial information
- Any sensitive data categories defined in GDPR Article 9

vPLAAF does not perform profiling or automated decision making.

# 5. How We Collect Data

Data is collected via:

- Membership application/registration forms
- Direct submission from members
- Publicly accessible VATSIM API data
- Discord server membership

Website visitors may browse public content anonymously.

# 6. Purposes of Data Processing

### 6.1 Membership Administration

- Identity verification
- Roster maintenance
- Communication with members
- Tracking pilot qualifications and training progress

### 6.2 Operational Requirements

- Participation in VSOA-compliant activities
- Event scheduling and coordination
- Training program records for audit compliance

### 6.3 Communications

- Event announcements
- Safety notices
- Administrative updates

### 6.4 Cookies (Website)

If used, cookies serve only the following purposes:

- Keeping users logged in
- Saving website preferences

No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

# 7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

vPLAAF **does not sell, trade, or commercially share** personal data.

Data may be shared only under the following circumstances:

### 7.1 VATSIM or VSOA Governance Bodies

Only when required for:

- Compliance verification
- Safety review
- Organizational audit

### 7.2 Service Providers

Limited to platforms used for operations, such as:

- Google Forms
- Website hosting providers
- Discord

These services process data solely as instructed and provide GDPR-equivalent protections.

### 7.3 Public Information Requirements

To comply with VSOA policies, vPLAAF may publicly display:

- First name + last initial
- VATSIM CID
- Pilot status (active/inactive)

Email addresses and internal records are **never made public**.

# 8. International Data Transfers

vPLAAF uses cloud-based services that may store data outside the EU/EEA on GDPR Chapter V.

By submitting data, members consent to international transfers subject to:

- Adequate safeguards
- Standard Contractual Clauses (where applicable)
- GDPR-equivalent protection standards by service providers

vPLAAF does not share data with any government or commercial entity.

# 9. Data Retention

vPLAAF retains personal data only as long as necessary:

- Active members: retained for the duration of membership
- Former members: retained up to **12 months** for organizational integrity
- Training logs: retained up to **36 months** for audit purposes

After this period, data is securely deleted.

Members may request earlier deletion at any time.

# 10. Data Subject Rights

By GDPR Chapter III, you have the following rights:

- **Right to be informed**
- **Right of access** to your personal data
- **Right to rectification**
- **Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)**
- **Right to restrict processing**
- **Right to withdraw consent**
- **Right to data portability** (on request)

A deletion request will terminate vPLAAF membership.

To exercise these rights, contact the Data Controller.
